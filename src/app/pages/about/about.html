WHO WE ARE

Roomzo makes finding rooms, PGs, and flats clearer and more direct.

Roomzo (roomzo.in) is a technology platform for rental listings. Owners publish rooms, paying guest stays, and flats; seekers browse by city and category, read details, and contact owners by call or WhatsApp after a short safety reminder. We are not a brokerage firm and we do not claim that every offline deal is risk-free — we focus on useful discovery tools and practical safety guidance.

Why Roomzo exists

Renting in India often involves brokers, opaque fees, and pressure to pay before seeing a property. Students moving to Prayagraj or Varanasi, and professionals relocating to Pune or Lucknow, need a simple way to discover options and speak to the person responsible for the property. Roomzo was built for that flow: search → compare → contact → visit → decide.

What we do

  • Host owner-submitted listings with photos, rent, location, and amenities.
  • Offer city pages, category guides, and search filters so users can explore meaningfully.
  • Enable call and WhatsApp contact with safety messaging (visit before you pay; watch for scam patterns).
  • Provide report tools when a listing appears fraudulent or incorrect.
  • Publish educational blog and FAQ content about renting safely.

What we do not do

  • We do not guarantee listing accuracy — owners are responsible for their content.
  • We do not hold deposits, execute rent agreements, or transfer property keys on your behalf.
  • We do not promise “zero scam” outcomes; safety depends on user caution and offline verification.

Where we focus

Active discovery today emphasises cities such as Prayagraj, Varanasi, Pune, and Lucknow, with more cities planned as inventory grows. Both students and working professionals use Roomzo, as do owners who want inbound interest without listing only through agents.
